Services

No matter where the inspiration derives from for your next look, be it television, film, or the runway, Del Beauty Box is a hair salon that has the capability to get you there with confidence, creating a look that will meet and exceed your expectations. Using only the best products available in the industry, we ensure that your hair, face and body receive the best nourishment possible to maintain your look for as long as possible. Once you make an appointment at our beauty salon , we will give you expert advice on the best products for your particular hair to produce a singular look that works for you in every way imaginable. Del Beauty Bar delivers by giving our clients the quintessential look of distinction that every one wants and deserves. Del Beauty Box goes even further with their beauty services, offering you the best in bridal and special events assistance as well for those special occasions. Don't leave your big day to chance when you can receive a complimentary consultation from one of our elite stylists, who will go over a range of looks to complement you that will fit the tone for the event you are preparing for. At Del Beauty Box, we offer a wide range of services and products to help you achieve a hue that is totally you. From basic root tints to highlights, outer colors or even corrective coloring, we take your hair and revitalize it, infusing it with just the right products and precision cutting to keep you up to date on the latest styles trending. So no matter what your hair color or type may be, we can take your locks and style, texture, layer, curl, and straighten to perfection, always using the best products and tools to create your final look and we are confident that we will make you look Great.
